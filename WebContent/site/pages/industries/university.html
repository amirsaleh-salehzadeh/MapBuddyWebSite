Geofencing Educational Facilities

Demonstrate the virtual perimeter of the spaces belonging to the institution. Map Buddy allows for managing a geofence by generating the radius around a targeted space dynamically and editing the boundaries of the space. Hereby, the managers will be able to position the facilities, services and spaces on the map.

For instance, campuses, sport, healthcare and restaurant facilities, as well as the parking areas and buildings can be shown on the map.

This improves the student experience by reducing stress and uncertainty. Include links to meeting rooms directly in the invitation and timetables to facilitate finding a destination.

Positioning Indoor Facilities

In an economical manner, Map Buddy allows for scaling and digitising the floor plans of a building. The information of a plan components can be also edited and presented to the students and visitors in a user-friendly way.
You just need few clicks to edit the information of components, facilities and services that you provide inside a building.

For instance, the indoor position of classrooms, seminars, offices and restrooms, as well as computer labs, print facilities and fire suppression systems can be shown on a floor plan.

It keeps the information updated and reduces the amount of time required for finding people, places, and facilities. Furthermore, it enables the new visitors to get familiar with a building.

User Management

Map Buddy allows the decision makers of your institution to manage users and their access to the system functionalities

For instance, campus, building, level and room manager, as well as the QR code printer and printer repair technician.

To facilitate communication between users & management of the spaces & activities.

Indoor & Outdoor Navigation

Digitise and manage the paths between the components of your space. It allows the Map Buddy to navigate the students to a destination based on the transport mode.
By using the Map Buddy you only need to allocate the width of a path, and connect two points together just with a few clicks.

For instance, digitise the paths through stairways, elevators, one-way, two-way roads as well as, the paths which the users on a wheelchair can cross.

Map Buddy navigates the users based on a sequence of transport modes. For instance, for a user who drives a car, Map Buddy navigates the user to the closest parking, before guiding the user to the destination.

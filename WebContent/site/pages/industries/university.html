Geofencing Educational Facilities
Demonstrate the virtual perimeter of the spaces belonging to the institution. Map Buddy allows for managing a geofence by generating the radius around a targeted space dynamically and editing the boundaries of the space. Hereby, the managers will be able to position the facilities, services and spaces on the map.

For instance, campuses, sport, healthcare and restaurant facilities, as well as the parking areas and buildings can be shown on the map.

Improve the student experience by reducing stress and uncertainty.
Include links to meeting rooms directly in the invitation and timetables to facilitate finding a destination.

Positioning Indoor Facilities

In an economical manner, Map Buddy allows for scaling and digitising the floor plans of a building. The information of a plan components can be also edited and presented to the students and visitors in a user-friendly way. You just need few clicks to edit the information of components, facilities and services being provided inside a building.

For instance, the indoor position of classrooms, seminars, offices and restrooms, as well as computer labs, print facilities and fire suppression systems can be shown on a floor plan.

Keep the information updated and reduce the amount of time required for finding people, places, and facilities.
Enable new visitors to experience the buildings before being there.

Indoor & Outdoor Navigation

Digitise and manage the paths between the components of your space enables measuring the distance and navigating the students to a destination based on the transport mode. Using Map Buddy, you can edit the width of paths and connect two points together just with few clicks.

For instance, Enables digitising the paths through stairways, elevators, one-way, two-way roads as well as, the paths which the users riding a wheelchair or bicycle can cross.

Navigate the users based on a sequence of transport modes. For instance, for a user who drives a car, navigate the user to the closest parking, before guiding the user to the destination.

Tracking Users & Collecting Movement Data

Map Buddy uses the GPS to track the users and movable items within the outdoor areas and uses QR codes to enable tracking through the indoor areas.

Although, the users & items can be tracked by other tracking technologies, including, WIFI, Bluetooth Beacons, RFID and camera.

Map Buddy collects real-time data of movements and respectively facilitates managing the movement of users.
Therefore, the accurate location of the people and movable objects (i.e. school bus & food trucks) can be shared with the users just-in-time.

Lift Sharing

Map Buddy uses the GPS technology to track the transport services of an institution and share this information with the users. The reservation system enables the registered & certified users to reserve a seat, however, by integrating the timetable of your institution, this process can be automated. Map Buddy also enables the users to share a lift when they drive from a departure to your destination.

This allows for scheduling the timetable of the transport services dynamically. Reduces the vehicle's unhealthful ambient CO emissions. Creates a strong sustainability culture. Reduces the number of single driver vehicles in the area. Creates an extra income for the drivers & reduces the costs of a trip.

Digitising & Digitalising Things

By digitising the assets, the position & information of an asset can be shown and monitored on the map in real-time. Digitalising the assets enables involving them in the business process of your institution. The information from ambient sensors & wearable sensory device of users can be integrated with the business process of your institution.

For instance, office facilities, computer hardware & software devices, inventories in the warehouse & advanced lab equipment can all be digitised & digitalised. Moreover, the light, oxygen, humidity sensors and cameras installed in a room, as well as the smart watches of the students can be integrated.

Facilitates tracking, reserving & monitoring the movement of assets. Enables inter-departmental transparency of the assets & facilitates communication. Improves demanding for a maintenance or service related to an asset just-in-time. Using the ambient sensors the energy consumption of a space, the operation occurring in it & the occupancy of it can be monitored. Using the information from the smart devices of the visitors & students, their movement, current activity and even cognitive & emotional states can be tracked & monitored. This can incredibly improve the quality of student's learning & stress level.
